Fiber Laser
Description

A laser marking machine designed to create precise and permanent marks on various metals, such as steel, aluminum, brass, glass and plastics (jobs created by plastic molding)

Color Sublimation

Hot Press
Description

Color sublimation is a heat-transfer printing technique that embeds vibrant, long-lasting designs onto various materials like fabrics and frames. It is commonly used on polyester and synthetic fabrics for clothing, ensuring fade-resistant prints.

Similarly, it is applied to coated metal and other surfaces for frames, creating high-quality, durable images for decorative and personalized items.
