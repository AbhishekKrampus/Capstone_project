Laser Marking

Fiber laser marking is a high-precision, non-contact marking technique widely used in industrial applications for engraving, etching, and marking on a variety of materials. It uses a fiber optic-based laser source to deliver concentrated energy to mark the surface of an object.

Color Sublimation

Color sublimation, also known as sublimation printing, is a digital printing technique that transfers designs onto materials using heat and pressure. This process relies on the sublimation of dye, where solid dyes are converted directly into a gaseous state without passing through the liquid phase, bonding with the surface of the substrate.
